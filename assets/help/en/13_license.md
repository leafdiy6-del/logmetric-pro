# Chapter 12 — License

---

## 1. Free Trial

After first installing LogMetric Pro, the program automatically activates a **30-day free trial**.

- No registration or activation code required
- **Almost all features** are available during the trial
- A reminder appears on each launch showing remaining trial days

> **Note:** Cloud backup is a full-version exclusive; it is not available during the trial.

---

## 2. Checking License Status

**Path:** Settings → About → View Basic Info

The popup shows:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| License Status | Trial / Full version |
| Expiry Date | Trial or full license validity period |
| Contact Email | Purchase inquiry contact (copy with one tap) |
| Device Fingerprint | Unique device identifier (copy with one tap) |

The "About" section in Settings also displays the current status directly, e.g.:

```
View Basic Info          Trial · 18 days remaining  >
```

---

## 3. Purchasing the Full Version

When the trial expires, the program shows a lock screen. You can purchase directly from the lock screen.

Full-version exclusive features:
- ☁️ **Cloud Backup**: automatic data backup to the cloud — restore seamlessly when switching devices

**Purchase process:**

1. The lock screen displays your **Device Fingerprint** — tap to copy it
2. Send an email to **leaff.doo@icloud.com** with the device fingerprint and your purchase intent
3. Complete payment (**€150 / year**)
4. Receive an activation code (format: `PRO-XXXX-XXXX`)
5. On the lock screen, tap **Enter Activation Code** and input the code you received
6. The program unlocks immediately and restores full functionality

---

## 4. Renewal

When the full version expires:

1. Contact **leaff.doo@icloud.com** with your renewal intent
2. After payment is confirmed, **no action is needed on your end** — the program automatically restores within 5 minutes

> No need to re-enter the activation code; the system detects and renews automatically.

---

## 5. Switching Devices

After switching devices:

- The new device automatically gets a 30-day free trial
- To continue with your full version, contact **leaff.doo@icloud.com** with the new device's fingerprint to re-bind the license

---

## 6. FAQ

**Q: Will my data be lost after the trial ends?**
A: No. All entered data is preserved in full. Simply purchase to restore access immediately.

**Q: How many devices can one license cover?**
A: A license is bound to one device. For multiple devices, please contact us to discuss.

**Q: How long does it take for a renewal to take effect?**
A: After payment confirmation, the program automatically detects and restores access within 5 minutes — no action needed.

**Q: How do I confirm I already have the full version?**
A: Settings → About — if it shows "Full Version · Expiry Date", the license is active.

**Q: Is my data still there after the program locks?**
A: Yes. The lock only pauses use; all data remains intact and immediately accessible after purchase.
