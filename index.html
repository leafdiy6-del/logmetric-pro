🇪🇺 EUDR-Ready Documentation

The EU Forest Law Enforcement, Governance and Trade (FLEGT) regulation requires that timber imports carry proof of origin, species, and volume. LogMetric Pro's PDF reports are built to meet this standard.

  • Measurer name & signature field
  • Company details (seller / buyer)
  • Wood species (scientific name)
  • Volume by grade with deduction applied
  • Container number & date
  • No third-party data sharing — your data stays yours

Real-time Error Detection

Enter 450 instead of 4.50? The app turns the row red instantly — wrong decimal or oversized log caught before it's too late.

🏷️

Dual Tag — Split Log Tracking

When a log is cut but not yet separated, mark both entries as "Dual Tag." The system counts it as 1 piece and accumulates volume — no more double-counting.

✅

Built-in Verification

Compare your entries against the counterparty's sheet — list mode for fast batch check, or card-by-card mode for careful reconciliation. Mark rows verified or flagged.

📲

One-tap Handover (.lmp)

Field crew measures half a container, then hits "Handover Share." The .lmp file goes to the next shift — they open it and continue from exactly where you left off.

⚖️

Weight Estimation

Select wood species for density, set moisture content (MC). The app estimates total weight — useful for transport quotes and pricing reference.

📊

Live Bottom Stats Bar

As you add each log, the bottom bar updates instantly: grade counts, short-logs, thin-logs, and total volume — no tab-switching required.

📏

Length & Diameter Deductions

Set defect deductions for length and diameter. The app calculates net volume — accurate for bark removal, end-checks, or any commercial agreement rule.

Chinese / English / Slovenian

Full interface in all three languages. Perfect for cross-border operations where field crews and office staff speak different languages.

FAQ

Common questions.

What's the difference between the trial and the paid version?
Nothing — the trial gives you access to all features for 30 days. After purchase your license is simply extended. No data migration needed.
Which measurement standards does the app support?
13 standards total: Huber EN 1309-2, Czech ČSN 48 0009, Hoppus, NF B53-020, JAS, Ontario, MARN, D²L (metric), and Doyle, Roy, Scribner Decimal C, International 1/4", GOST 2708-75, ISO 4480-83 (imperial/BF). Switching between standards recalculates all entries instantly.
Is cloud backup secure?
Your data is stored in Supabase, a GDPR-compliant cloud platform. Only you can access your data with your license. The app does not share any data with third-party advertisers or analytics services.
Does the app work offline?
Yes — all measurement entry, calculation, and local export work fully offline. Cloud backup requires an internet connection. Local auto-backup also works offline and stores files on your device or in iCloud Drive.
Can I transfer my license to a new device?
Yes. Your license is tied to your device fingerprint. Contact leaff.doo@icloud.com with your new device's code to re-bind your license — no extra charge for the same user.
Does the app support imperial (Board Foot) standards?
Yes. When a BF formula like Doyle or International 1/4" is selected, the input fields switch to feet and inches automatically. Results display in BF. When a metric formula is selected, fields return to meters and centimeters.
