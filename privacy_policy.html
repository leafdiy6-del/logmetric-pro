本隐私政策适用于由 Leaff d.o.o. 开发的 LogMetric Pro 应用程序（以下简称"本应用"）。我们非常重视您的隐私，本文件将说明我们如何收集、使用和保护您的数据。

2. 我们收集的数据

数据类型存储位置用途

  • 相机或麦克风数据
  • 设备联系人或通讯录
  • 广告标识符
  • 任何用于广告目的的个人信息

本应用使用 Supabase（supabase.com）处理许可证验证及云备份存储。Supabase 是符合 GDPR 的云服务提供商。云备份数据在上传前已在设备本地完成 AES-256-CBC 加密，Supabase 及开发者均无法读取其内容。您可查阅 Supabase 隐私政策了解其数据处理方式。除此之外，本应用不集成任何广告 SDK、分析工具或社交平台 SDK。

5. 数据保留

This Privacy Policy applies to LogMetric Pro, developed by Leaff d.o.o. ("we", "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This document explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses Supabase (supabase.com) for license verification and cloud backup storage. Supabase is a GDPR-compliant cloud provider. Cloud backup data is AES-256-CBC encrypted on the device before upload; neither Supabase nor the developer can read its contents. Please refer to Supabase's Privacy Policy for details on their data handling. We do not integrate any advertising SDKs, analytics platforms, or social media SDKs.

5. Data Retention

  • Local data: Stored on your device; deleted when you uninstall the app.
  • License data: Retained on Supabase until you request deletion.

7. Children's Privacy

LogMetric Pro is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
